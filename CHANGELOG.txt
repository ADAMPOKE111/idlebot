### Version 1.1

* Added steam chat control/interactivitiy such as "/change" (change idling appID), "/name" (change steam name of bot) and "idle" to check if/what the bot is idling.
* Added variables:
	appID (can independantly control appID - may be used in the future)
	newappID (holds the new appID to idle via /change)
	newName (holds the new steam name to change to via /name)
